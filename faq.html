Do we need to apply before placing a deposit?

Yes. Families should submit an application first. Deposits will only be accepted after the application has been reviewed and approved.

Are deposits refundable?

Deposit terms should be clearly confirmed before payment.

How do we ask about availability?

Availability updates can be handled through the application form, direct contact, or our dedicated litter update section.
