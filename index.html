Welcome To The Farm

A calm country place for families, animals, and memory-making.

Cavalier Country Farm is growing into a family-centered farm experience built around gentle animals, peaceful scenery, and honest country hospitality.

From Cavaliers and miniature horses to future petting zoo visits and photography sessions, the farm is being shaped as a place where families can slow down, connect with animals, and enjoy the beauty of life outdoors.

Future Petting Zoo

A gentle farm experience for children and families.

As this offering develops, this section can grow into hours, visit rules, ticket information, animal safety guidelines, birthday party options, and seasonal farm events.
