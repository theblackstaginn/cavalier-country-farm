Welcome To Cavalier Country Farm

Nestled beside the water beneath towering trees, Cavalier Country Farm is home to Cavaliers, miniature horses, playful goats, and peaceful moments surrounded by nature.

Our farm is centered around calm country living, thoughtful animal care, and sharing the beauty of life on the farm with others.

Life On The Farm

From quiet mornings by the lake to evenings spent with the animals beneath glowing sunsets, every corner of the farm is shaped by warmth, family, and connection to nature.

Cavaliers nap beside miniature horses, goats climb everything they can find, and every season brings a new rhythm to life here at the farm.
